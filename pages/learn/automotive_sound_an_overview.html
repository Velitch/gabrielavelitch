AUTOMOTIVE IMMERSIVE SOUND

An Overview

The purpose of this handbook is to bring notions, tips and possible solutions litteraly to the hand of the sound engineers while approaching to the automotive immersive sound tuning. This text is an historycal and technical summary of the most relevant articles, books, papers and general notions related to acoustics, sound engineering and sound design. We will frequently refer to the material mentioned above; it's not a purpose of this book to leave the readers fully informed about all the touched topics, infact we will frequenctly redirect them to the original source for futher studies. On the other hand, this text will focus over results, conclusions and solutions in order to give an immediate view of the state of arts and to provide a practical approach to the matter.
