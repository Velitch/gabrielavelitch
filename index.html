Tribute to Mario Bertoncini

Concert and Masterclass

The star of the evening is the quartet that Mario Bertoncini himself founded shortly before his death. The program includes two of his works, together with works by Walter Branchi and Giorgio Nottoli (world premiere dedicated to Mario, for the quartet). The concert ends with Improvviso - subtitle by/for Mario Bertoncini, a performance in which the Maestro's sound sculptures preserved in the Bertoncini Fund recently established at the Isabella Scelsi Foundation are used extensively.

Dual Channel DI Box

Instruments Developement

The DI Box is a indispensable tool in any concert or studio context. The Dual Channel DI Box was made on commission: the customer needed a compact, versatile device intended for the most varied uses. The tool makes use of the best components available on the market. The Monacor 10:1 (DIB-110) transformer is the star of the system! Its performances are currently the best in its category and make this product satisfactory in use. The tailor-made design of the product is what makes the difference, it's your idea that that becomes reality!

Centro Ricerche Musicali

Art Installations

A non-profit cultural centre, CRM was founded at Rome in 1988 by composers Laura Bianchini and Michelangelo Lupone with a group of musicians and researchers for the purpose of promoting research into the aesthetic, analytical, musicological and scientific aspects of music. In 1990 CRM was officially recognized as a “Music Research Centre” by the Ministry for University Education and Scientific & Technological Research and subsequently, in 1993, by the Japan Foundation.
